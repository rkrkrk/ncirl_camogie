Camogie Skills

Camogie is a very skillful game. You need to learn the correct techniques and then develop your abilities through practice, practice and more practice. The latest research into what makes some people good at skillful sports suggest that so called "natural talent" doesn't exist. By for the most important factor is the number of hours you have spend practicing.

Training twice a week and a match at the weekend is not enough, you need to be practicing on your own at home every day. Have a look at the skills videos below to perfect your technique and get ideas for drills you can do in your back garden or against a gable wall.

In addition to skills you also need to develop tactical awareness, correct positioning and an ability to read the game. Review the tactics videos below.

Tactics

In addition to practice and developing skills you should try to develop an ability to read the game and understand tactics. Have an awareness of what is happening around you, support your team mates when they have the ball and give them options for passes. When defending, watch your marking and try to cover for other players when they are pulled out of position. Study the videos below. [more...] [more...]

Defending - Covering / Marking

º Be alert and react to other players being pulled out of position.
º Cover back to mark free players.
º Talk to each other

Defending - Positions / Marking

º Don't over commit to having too many defenders trying to block or win ball leaving forwards unmarked.
º Don't bunch up too much.
º Push out to cover unmarked forwards.

Supporting player in possesion

º Move into space ready to receive pass once possesion is won
º Move away from your marker
º Call for pass when you are free
º Run to support the player in possesion
